Nonprofit Website Redesign

Rape Recovery Center / raperecoverycenter.org


UX/UI Designers:
Arielle Moore, Jenny Clausen & Kaitlyn Clarke

Overview


The Rape Recovery Center (RRC) is a non-profit center in Salt Lake City that provides help and resources for victims of rape and sexual abuse.

The RRC provides:


    24-hour crisis line
    Support for survivors and their families
    Resources and education for both survivors, their loved ones, and volunteers

Problem Statement


Survivors coming to the RRC's website need to quickly and easily find the help that they need. Volunteers need to be able to find resources and clear instructions for how they can help support survivors and become an advocate for others.


How might we make the RRC website more clear and simple, and make their important information as accessible as possible?
